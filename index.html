05

Data notes & pipeline

How the numbers are made, and the caveats worth knowing.

Coverage. A weekly GitHub Action scrapes menu HTML; the analysis pipeline reprocesses the full corpus on demand. These figures run through 2026-04-05.

Menu boilerplate. The newer site layout interleaves price lines, section headers and greetings into menu blocks. The cleaning step now filters these (~40 strings, ~1,600 occurrences) so they no longer rank as "dishes".

Categorizer quirks. Course/protein tags are heuristic — galuska is sometimes filed as a soup, meat sides leak into főzelék. Per-dish protein/method are noisy and omitted here; the explorer relies on the more reliable course and season facets.
